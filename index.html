Old History | Things that happened long time ago

I was born in 30th December, 1994. As a slip says, this happened in Patan Hospital. Patan Hospital is kind of a reputed hospital so, I guess proper care was given to me/my mom during my birth. I was roughly 2.5 kg at birth. My childhood was pretty normal, it seems. As per my parents, I did take T.B. medicine for 9 months when I was roughly 3 years old.

General Medical History

I used to fall ill rarely, most of the times being due to stomach. I had fevers, both the shivering and non-shivering kind at times. Most of them lasted 1 week maximum. I was diagnosed of typhoid in recent years.

Seizure Related History

I used to experienced some dizziness some months back. Developed confusions (I used to go rounds while walking), which were usually followed by unusual feelings that I used to develop.

Allergic to Phenytoin | Discontinued It's Use

After some weeks from my first attack, I developed some rashes in my body. I then consulted same doctor, Dr. Niranjan Acharya and was found allergic to Phenytoin. He advised me to consult Dermaetologist and stop the medication for 1 weeks (or so). I did as suggested. I used _______ medicines for 1 weeks for my rashes, and stopped taking phenytoin.

After two weeks, I planned to change my regular doctor to Dr. Basanta Panta. I told him that I wanted to stop my medication if it was possible to do so. The doctor agreed and we discussed that it was not necessary to continue the medication at this stage. The medicine (Phenytoin) was stopped. I was prescribed with "Vitamin" (in doctor's word).

Before I developed rashes, I did have some unusual feelings occasionally or rarely. I also had explained this to my new doctor. I was kinda relieved that I would not have to take those medicines now.
